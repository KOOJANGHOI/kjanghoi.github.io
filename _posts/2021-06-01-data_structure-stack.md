### Stack ADT

a list that insertions and deletions can be performed at the end of the list 

#### operations
- Push(X, S): insert X in the list S 
- Pop(S): deletes the most recently inserted element from S
- CreateStack(S)
- MakeEmpty(S)
- Top(S) : returns top element of stack S
